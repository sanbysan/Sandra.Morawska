About me

If I were to describe myself in three words, it would be pragmatic, organized and creative. Privately I'm a vegan and animal lover. I love dogs and spending time with them, which I easily combine with passion for sport and physical activity. My dogs, sport and passion for vegan cuisine fill my free time.
